Features

Everything You Need
In One Platform

A comprehensive suite of tools designed to make academic management effortless for students, faculty, and administrators.

Smart Course Registration

Browse, search, and filter courses by department, semester, or credits. Enroll instantly with real-time seat availability tracking.

Result Management

Faculty can upload internal & external marks, auto-calculate grades. Students can view semester-wise results with GPA/CGPA.

Role-Based Access

Three distinct portals — Student, Faculty, and Admin — each with tailored dashboards, features, and secure authentication.

Live Dashboard

Real-time statistics, animated counters, interactive charts, and recent activity feeds to keep everyone informed at a glance.

PDF Transcripts

Students can download beautifully formatted academic transcripts as PDF documents with a single click.

Dark Mode

Complete dark/light theme support throughout the entire application, with smooth transitions and persistent user preference.
